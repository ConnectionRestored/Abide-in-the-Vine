$$\frac{1}{f} = \frac{1}{s_o} + \frac{1}{s_i}$$
With:
- $s_o$ = object distance
- $s_i$ = image distance
- $f$ = focal length

Results:
| Variable | Positive (+) | Negative (-)  |
|:--------:|:------------:|:-------------:|
|   $f$    |   concave    |    convex     |
|  $s_i$   |  real image  | virtual image |

**Notice:** A diverging mirror always has a negative $f$.
